About

Buddies for Life, Inc. is a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization that began in 2003 for the purpose of finding homes for homeless dogs and puppies and reducing the number of healthy animals that are euthanized by raising public awareness about the importance of spay/neuter. We have rescued over 1500 dogs, puppies, cats, and kittens and have helped finance the spaying/neutering of many more. We work with several compassionate veterinarians in the Orlando area to offer low-cost spay/neuter. If you are interested in having your dog or cat spayed/neutered at low cost ($25), please find the form at http://fixfido.weebly.com.
